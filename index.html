Our St George Bisons

Bison are known for their massive size, thick fur, and incredible strength, making them one of the most iconic animals of North America. Despite their heavy build, they can move quickly and survive harsh conditions by using their strong heads to push through snow in search of food. These animals play an important role in maintaining healthy grasslands and have become a lasting symbol of resilience and the American West. In the St. George area, small protected herds have been maintained, with an estimated population of around 150 bison in nearby regions. Today, they continue to represent both the natural environment and the history of the region.
